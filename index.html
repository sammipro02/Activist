Autism's Impact

1 in 54 people in the U.S. have autism and can be negatively affected by it in school. This can happen in many ways. Some ways are by bullying, being held back academically, and more. But, together, we can change that. Schools need to provide more opportunities for students with disabilites, specifically people with autism.
